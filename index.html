About the Project

Brains and Bytes is a science outreach project created to introduce high school and university students to the connection between the brain and technology. The goal is to show, in a simple and fun way, how computers and engineering tools can help us study how the brain works.

Through short talks, interactive activities, and live demonstrations with EEG, students will see how brain signals can be recorded and turned into digital information. The event encourages curiosity about how the mind works, helps students understand the basics of brain activity, and shows how neuroscience and computer science can work together to create new ideas and technologies.
